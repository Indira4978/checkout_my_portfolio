About Me

Bonjour! Iam Indira. I was born in the French Caribbeans and raised in East of France. I spent years in two vibrant cities, London and Dubai, where I honed my skills as a sommelier. My journey in the world of fine wines has been an incredible adventure, allowing me to explore the diverse and ever-evolving landscape of the beverage industry. As much as I cherish the art of wine, I've found a new passion that excites me - front-end development. I've delved into the world of coding and web design, constantly learning and exploring the vast possibilities of creating beautiful and functional websites.
